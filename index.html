Interchain GmbH

Hello! We're Interchain GmbH, a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Interchain Foundation. As maintainers of the Tendermint project, lead architects of the Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC) Protocol, and contributors to the Cosmos SDK, we envision a more open, accountable, and self-sovereign world made possible through the Cosmos Network.

We are a group of engineers and protocol architects, formerly employed at All In Bits (d.b.a Tendermint Inc), who have recently branched out as Interchain GmbH to focus on supporting the core technologies behind the Internet of Blockchains. We'll continue to collaborate with our friends and colleagues at All In Bits and elsewhere in the Cosmos ecosystem.
